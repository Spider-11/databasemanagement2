UNLIKE PREDEFINED EXCEPTIONS, USER-DEFINED EXCEPTIONS ARE DECLARED AND DEFINED BY THE USER. USER-DEFINED EXCEPTIONS ARE DECLARED IN THE DECLARATION SECTION OF PL/SQL BLOCK WITH THEIR TYPE AS EXCEPTION.

declare 
        e emp%rowtype; 
        nd exception; 
        pragma exception_init(nd,-01422); 
        begin 
        select * into e from emp; 
        dbms_output.put_line('hello'); 
        exception 
         
        when nd then 
            dbms_output.put_line('too many rows are there'); 
            when others then 
            dbms_output.put_line('no data available'); 
        end; 
    

        Statement processed.
        too many rows are there
