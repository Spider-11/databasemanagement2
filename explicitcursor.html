EXPLICIT CURSORS ARE DECLARED EXPLICITLY BY THE USER, ALONG WITH OTHER IDENTIFIERS TO BE USED IN A PL/SQL BLOCK. THESE ARE ALSO KNOWN AS USER-DEFINED CURSORS, DEFINED IN THE DECLARE SECTION OF THE PL/SQL BLOCK.

declare 
        cursor cur is select * from student; 
        x student%rowtype; 
        begin 
        open cur; 
        loop 
        fetch cur into x; 
        exit when(cur%notfound); 
        dbms_output.put_line(x.name); 
        end loop; 
        close cur; 
        end;
