NOT IN OPERATOR IS INVERSE OF AN NOT OPERATOR.

mysql> select * from student_marks where age not in
        -> (select age from student_marks where age>19);
    +--------+------+-------------+
    | rollno | age  | total_marks |
    +--------+------+-------------+
    |   4264 |   19 |          90 |
    |   4269 |   19 |          70 |
    +--------+------+-------------+
    2 rows in set (0.09 sec)
